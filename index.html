THE MANIFESTO - WHY THIS EXISTS

The world has moved. Most workforce planning hasn't.

A significant proportion of tasks in a typical mid-market organisation can be meaningfully redesigned using current AI capability - yet fewer than 8% of CHROs have mapped which tasks those are.

None of the top 20 HR technology platforms show a board the financial case for workforce transformation before the programme begins.

The CHRO's challenge is not that AI exists. It is that no one has shown them exactly what to do with it.
